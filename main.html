As a family owned company, Alamo Food Equipment & Supplies, Inc. has acquired distributorships from the finest manufacturers of food processing equipment. Bringing these companies under our logo gives our customers a unique ability to purchase from one source, while still getting the "right" equipment for the job.

Our customers like the low-key professional approach that our salesmen use. Sales representatives are well aware of the latest equipment and our trained service technicians provide prompt attention when a problem does occur. The commitment doesn't end there. Special emphasis and pride have built a service/maintenance division second to none. We understand what service means to our customers. With this in mind, we offer 24 hour-7 days a week service. With 7 technicians servicing the greater San Antonio and Austin areas, and 2 technicians servicing Laredo and the Valley, factory support is just a phone call away.

Applicants


Service :

We are looking for an aggressive, yet honest, person to service food-processing equipment. Must have experience in repairing mechanical and electrical equipment. Applicant will be working on equipment found in meat markets and other food-processing facilities. Must have a clean driving record! We have been in business for over 35 years and are well-established in the food-processing industry. Experience in the food-processing industry is a plus, but not a requirement. A company vehicle is provided along with paid holidays, vacation/sick pay, 401K, and medical insurance. We are a small, family-owned-and-operated business looking for a team player to be a part of our family. Please respond with your last three jobs, what you made at these jobs, and why you left. Also, please provide current salary requirements. Please e-mail gary@alamofoodequipment.com and copy your resume into the body of the email, not as an attachment.

Sales :

Looking for a sales representative to sell food-processing equipment and supplies throughout the state of Texas. Candidate needs to be able to make cold calls as well as taking care of existing clients. Applicant will also need to be able to make deliveries to customers on all products they sell (equipment & supplies). On the job training is provided. Prior experience in the industry is not mandatory, but definitely a plus. We have been in the business since 1979. We offer a full package of benefites including: medical, dental, 401K, and more! We offer a base salary plus commissions and you will be provided with a work vehicle. Please e-mail gary@alamofoodequipment.com and copy your resume into the body of the email, not as an attachment. Please include salary requirements, why you left your last 3 jobs, and what you made at those last 3 jobs.
